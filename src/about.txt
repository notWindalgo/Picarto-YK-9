YK-9 is a standalone application that allows song requests from your Picarto.tv chat</br>
</br>
Your <b>Username</b> must match your Picarto account name</br>
Your <b>Oauth</b> token must be generated from <a href="https://oauth.picarto.tv/chat/bot" target="_blank">this link</a> (https://oauth.picarto.tv/chat/bot)</br>
</br>
Commands:</br>
!sr &#60;link&#62;: Adds a youtube link to the playlist queue</br>
!skip: Skips the current song in the playlist</br>
</br>
